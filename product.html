Crafted to last forever.

Every Rion piece is hermetically sealed by hand in our Japanese atelier. Designed for a lifetime of daily wear.

i

Hand Sealed

A small hole is drilled into the jewel, hair is placed inside by hand, then sealed with a cap of the same material — permanently airtight.

ii

Water Resistant

Hermetically sealed — safe for daily wear, handwashing, and light rain.

iii

Photo Documented

We photograph before, during, and after sealing — sent to you via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email.

iv

Lifetime Service

Complimentary polishing in Japan, for as long as you own the piece.
