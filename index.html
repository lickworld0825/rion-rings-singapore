Our Story

Born from loss.
Made from love.

In 2011, our founder held his infant son for three days — and then said goodbye. In that grief, a question was born: how do we carry the people we love when they can no longer walk beside us?

He discovered memorial jewellery — a ring that could hold a piece of his son close to his heart forever. Seven years later, in 2018, he founded Rion — named after that son. Every piece we create begins not with gold, but with a story. Yours.

"Eternal love.
A bond that continues forever."

A strand of hair from someone dear — a child, a parent, a partner, a beloved pet — becomes a jewel you wear forever. Just 1cm. One strand. That is all we need to carry them with you, always.

Your questions, answered

Just 1cm in length, one strand. Even less is perfectly fine. For pet fur, the same — 1cm, one strand. We work with whatever you can provide, no matter how little.

A US-sized ring gauge (sizes 1 to 12, including half sizes) is included in your kit. Simply try each ring and tell us which fits best. If you already know your US ring size — for example "US 6½" — just tell us and you can skip the gauge.

We ship to Singapore — DDP, with no charges on delivery. We send a secure hair collection kit to your Singapore address via SingPost. Inside: a sealed envelope, bilingual instructions, and a prepaid return label. Drop it at any SingPost outlet — we handle all customs. Your finished jewel ships via DHL Express, fully insured. We also ship to other countries upon request — please message us via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email.

Yes. We photograph the hair before sealing, during sealing, and after — showing it fully enclosed and hermetically sealed. We send these photos to you via WhatsApp or email before shipping. Complete peace of mind.

Approximately 5.5 months in total. We send progress updates via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email throughout the entire process.

Yes, on select designs. Multiple people's hair can be sealed together in one jewel — including hair from family members and pets. Ask us which designs support this when you enquire.

None at all. All prices are DDP — Singapore import GST (9%) are prepaid by Rion via DHL and included in the displayed price. You pay nothing extra when your jewel arrives at your door.

Yes. Many clients commission pieces for beloved pets. The kit and process are identical — the instructions for pet fur are included. 1cm of fur is all we need.

Because each piece is made entirely to your personal order — incorporating your hair, your chosen design, and your approved specifications — we do not accept cancellations, returns, or refunds once production has begun. Before any work starts, we obtain your written approval of the design and materials. In case of a manufacturing defect on our part, please notify us within 3 days of receipt via WhatsApp, email, or Instagram DM — we will remake the piece at no charge. Refunds are not offered.

Any remaining hair — no matter how little — is returned to you in a dedicated container, together with your completed jewel. Nothing precious is ever discarded.

Your piece is solid K18 gold or Pt900 platinum — exceptionally durable for daily wear. Wipe gently with a soft cloth after wearing. Avoid chlorine, perfume direct contact, and hard impacts. We provide complimentary lifetime polishing service in Japan — contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email to arrange.

The hair chamber is hermetically sealed in Japan — water-resistant for daily life (handwashing, light rain). For longevity, we recommend removing before swimming pools (chlorine), hot springs, and saunas.
