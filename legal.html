Section 3

Privacy Policy

My Art Inc. (Rion) is committed to protecting your personal information. We operate under Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (個人情報保護法 / APPI) and respect Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

1. Information We Collect

For each order, we collect:

  • Name, shipping address, phone number, email (or WhatsApp number)
  • Hair provided by you for sealing in the jewel
  • Payment confirmation details (we do not store card numbers)
  • Photographs of the hair sealing process (shared with you, retained as production records)
  • Communication records via WhatsApp, Instagram, or email

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Manufacture and deliver your bespoke jewel
  • Send photo reports of the production process
  • Handle DHL shipping and Singapore customs declarations
  • Issue invoices and process payments
  • Provide aftercare (lifetime polishing service)
  • Respond to your enquiries

3. Third-Party Sharing

We share information only with:

  • DHL Express — for international shipping and customs (name, address, phone)
  • Payment processors — for invoice settlement (encrypted)
  • Our Japanese artisans — for production (no addresses shared; only first names and order details)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

4. Hair Handling

Hair is treated with utmost respect and care. Excess hair remaining after sealing is returned to you with the finished piece in a dedicated container. Nothing is ever discarded.

5. Photography & Image Rights

Photographs taken during the sealing process and of the finished piece are copyrighted by My Art Inc. They are shared with you as part of the photo report for your records and personal viewing. You may share these photos on personal social media with attribution to Rion (@rion.rings_official). Use for commercial purposes, resale platforms, or competing brands' marketing materials requires prior written consent from Rion.

6. International Data Transfer (Singapore → Japan)

Your personal information is stored and processed at our atelier in Japan. By placing an order, you consent to the international transfer of your data from your country of residence (Singapore / elsewhere) to Japan. Japan is recognised as providing an adequate level of personal data protection under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) provides equivalent safeguards.

7. Data Retention

Order records (including photos of the sealing process) are retained for 10 years for warranty and aftercare purposes. You may request earlier deletion in writing.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for processing (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS, encrypted communications, and access-limited internal systems. Hair and physical orders are stored in our secured Japanese atelier.

10. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses minimal cookies for language preference. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

11. Contact for Privacy Concerns

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us via WhatsApp or at our registered address (see Business Information above).
