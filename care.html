Care Guide

Made to last
for generations.

Your Rion jewel is solid K18 gold or Pt900 platinum, hermetically sealed in Japan. With simple daily care, it will remain as beautiful as the day it was made — and far beyond.

The Rion Promise

Complimentary lifetime polishing.
Forever.

Every Rion piece comes with our lifetime maintenance service — free of charge, for as long as you own it. Polish, refresh, or refinish your jewel anytime by sending it to our Japanese atelier via DHL. We handle the rest.

Daily care.

Four simple habits to keep your jewel looking exceptional.

01
✦

Wipe after wearing

After each use, gently wipe your jewel with a soft, lint-free cloth. This removes natural skin oils, sweat, and dust — keeping the metal lustrous and the hair chamber clear.

02
◯

Store with care

Place your jewel in the Rion exclusive case when not worn. Avoid storing alongside other jewellery to prevent scratches. Keep away from direct sunlight and humidity.

03
◇

Remove for activities

Take off before swimming pools, hot springs, saunas, and intense workouts. Apply perfume, lotion, and sunscreen before putting on the jewel — never directly on it.

04
✧

Clean gently

For light cleaning, use a soft cloth or a gentle jewellery polishing cloth. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners and harsh chemicals. For deep cleaning, send to our Japanese atelier.

Do & Don't.

A quick reference for your everyday wear.

Do

  • Wipe with a soft cloth after each wear
  • Store in the Rion exclusive case
  • Remove before swimming, sauna, hot spring
  • Apply perfume / lotion before putting on
  • Send to Japan for free lifetime polishing
  • Wear daily — it is made for it

Don't

  • Expose to chlorine (pools, bleach)
  • Spray perfume directly on the jewel
  • Use ultrasonic cleaners
  • Use abrasive cloths or toothpaste
  • Knock against hard surfaces
  • Attempt to open the hair chamber
