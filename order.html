How to Order

Six simple
steps to your
forever jewel.

Every Rion piece is made entirely to your order. Our team guides you from first enquiry to doorstep delivery — at no extra cost. All prices are DDP: Singapore import GST are included.

Free DDP to Singapore WhatsApp support Photo updates

The journey — step by step

From choosing your design to receiving your jewel in Singapore, every step is guided by our team. It takes courage to begin — we make it easy.

i
Step One

Browse the Collection

Explore our series — from the Classic Ring (Series A) to the Statement Ring (Series J) — and choose the design that speaks to your heart. Each series is available in K18 Yellow Gold, White Gold, Pink Gold, and Platinum Pt900. If nothing quite fits, our fully bespoke option allows any design imaginable.

View the Collection →
ii
Step Two

Enquire via WhatsApp / DM / Email

Send us a message via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email with the series and material you are interested in. You are welcome to ask any question — about the design, the process, the sizing, engraving, or anything else. Our team responds within 24 hours, in English, Arabic, or Japanese.

No commitment required — just a conversation
iii
Step Three

Confirm Your Design & Pay

Once you are ready, we confirm all details together: series, material, ring size (a gauge is sent in your kit), engraving if desired, and the final DDP price. We then issue your invoice — payable by international bank transfer or credit card (Visa · Mastercard · American Express · Diners Club · JCB — via secure Stripe link). Full payment is required before production begins.

full payment before production · all prices DDP
iv
Step Four

Receive Your Hair Kit

We send you a Rion Hair Collection Kit by post — a sterile envelope with clear instructions and a return prepaid label. You simply place a few strands of hair inside (no cutting required — naturally shed hair is fine) and post it back to our atelier in Japan. The entire process takes just a few minutes.

Kit posted from Japan · return label included · any hair is accepted
v
Step Five

Crafted by Hand in Japan

Upon receiving your hair, our staff begins handcrafting your piece in our Japanese atelier. Each jewel takes approximately 5.5 months to complete. During this time, we send you a photo report documenting the hair sealing process — so you can witness every step of your jewel's creation.

Approx. 5.5 months · photo updates via WhatsApp / Instagram DM / email · 100% Made in Japan
vi
Step Six

Delivered to Your Door

Once your jewel passes our final quality inspection, we ship it to you via DHL Express — fully insured, with tracking. Delivery is DDP: all Singapore import GST are prepaid by Rion. Your only task is to open the door.

DHL Express · fully insured · DDP — no charges on arrival
