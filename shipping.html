How it works.

The complete shipping journey from order confirmation to delivery — entirely handled by Rion.

i

Hair collection kit ships from Japan

After your order is confirmed and invoice paid, we dispatch a secure hair collection kit to your Singapore address via SingPost. The kit includes a sealed envelope, ring gauge (US sizes 1–12), bilingual instructions, and a prepaid return label.

Japan → Singapore · Free
ii

You return the hair to us

Place the hair in the sealed envelope and drop the package at any post office (SingPost) using our prepaid label. All customs declarations are pre-completed by us — there is nothing for you to write or arrange.

Singapore → Japan · Free
iii

Crafting in our Japanese atelier

The hair arrives at our atelier in Japan. Crafting begins. We send photos at each milestone via WhatsApp — including before, during, and after the hair sealing process.

In Japan · Approx. 5.5 months
iv

Your finished jewel arrives

Shipped via DHL Global Services in a handcrafted Rion exclusive case with Certificate of Authenticity. Fully insured. DDP — all Singapore import GST (9%) prepaid by Rion via DHL. You pay nothing extra at the door.

Japan → Singapore · DDP · Insured

Returns & Defects.

Because every Rion piece is bespoke, please read these terms carefully before ordering.

Bespoke Order Policy

Each Rion jewel is made entirely to your personal order — incorporating your hair, your chosen design, and your approved specifications.

Before production begins, we obtain your written approval of the design, materials, and specifications. Once approved, production begins.

  • No cancellations after production begins
  • No returns on completed bespoke pieces
  • Invoice payment confirms order

Manufacturing Defects

If you discover a manufacturing defect on our part, we will remake the piece at no charge — including shipping and customs for the remake. Refunds are not offered.

Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or Instagram DM within 3 days of receiving your jewel with photos of the issue. We respond personally.

  • Free remake for manufacturing defects
  • All shipping & customs covered by Rion
  • Contact within 3 days of receipt
  • Lifetime polishing service complimentary

Questions about shipping?

Whether you're in Singapore or elsewhere — message us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through every step.
