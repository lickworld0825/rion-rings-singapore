Our Story

Born from loss.
Made with love.

Rion is not a brand. Rion is a son's name — and every jewel we make begins not with gold, but with someone you love.

Chapter One · 2011

A father. A son.
Three days.

In 2011, our founder lost his infant son. He held him in his arms for three days. Then he said goodbye.

In the silence that followed, a question emerged: how do we keep the people we love close when they can no longer walk beside us? He searched for a way to transform grief into something tangible. Not a brand. Not a luxury object. A vessel — something made from the hair of someone dear, worn close to the heart. A piece of them, forever.

He found memorial jewellery. And in finding it, he found his purpose.

"

A jewel is more than gold. It is a place where love can rest — and travel with you, always.

The Founder
Rion · Est. 2018, Japan

Chapter Two · 2018

Seven years later, Rion
was born.

For seven years, he studied. He visited artisans and learned the knowledge of Japanese goldsmithing passed down through generations. But he had to develop the technique himself — through countless trials, refinement, tears, and labor. He tested. He refined. He wept and he built.

In 2018, he founded Rion — named after his son. Rion is a brand, but not one built around gold or design. It is built around people. Every piece we create begins not with a material, but with someone you love: a parent, a partner, a child, a beloved pet. The jewel is the vessel. Love is what fills it.

Every piece we create is bespoke. Every piece is made entirely in Japan. Every piece holds a story that is yours alone.

Our Atelier

Where every jewel is made.

Our atelier is in Japan. Every piece — without exception — is crafted here. A skilled artisan personally oversees the sealing of every precious strand. There are no factories. There are no shortcuts. There is only craftsmanship, time, and care.

We work in K18 gold (yellow, white, pink), Pt900 platinum, and high-grade diamonds, rubies, and sapphires — all sourced within Japan. The sealing technique was perfected over years of iteration: each piece is hermetically sealed, designed to last for generations.

From the time a kit leaves our atelier to the moment your finished jewel returns to your hands, every step is touched by skilled hands. Your jewel is, quite literally, made by people who know your story — and care for it as if it were their own.
